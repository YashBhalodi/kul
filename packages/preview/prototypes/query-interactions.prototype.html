Lens × plate — how it works
Select
Click a person → the details widget, fixed to the right edge (it never covers the tree, so every card stays hoverable): detail on top, “Explore kin” opening the flat scrollable list. Its content follows the selection — and it has room to grow more affordances later.
Relate = hover (the lens)
While someone is selected, hovering any other card whispers the tie under it — the docked tag, live, zero clicks. The lens keeps reading during a kin-set paint.
One selection, always
There is no second selected state: clicking any person simply moves the selection (and the widget follows) — that’s also how you walk the family. Esc or a canvas click clears.
